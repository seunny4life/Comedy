Movies

The Film or Movie industry is one of the oldest industries in the world and it has contributed around over 100 Billions dollars to the world economy.
Film is used for a range of goals, including education and propaganda.

The world's oldest national film industry in the world and the largest in terms of box office gross revenue is the Hollywood industry of the USA while the largest national film industry in terms of numbers films or movies produced per year is India.

Since 2010, the highest annual ticket sales worldwide were Indian cinema with 3.5 billion tickets sold, followed by Hollywood with over 2.6 billion tickets sold.
